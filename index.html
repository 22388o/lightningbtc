How Does This Work?

If you have a Bitcoin Lightning Node and you want to open channels to have both outgoing and incoming capacity, we can help you. Here you can easily join a so called liquidity swap where multiple users like you open channels to each other. This setup results in a free incoming channel to you in return for you opening one to someone else. The process takes 4 simple steps. We will give you instructions all throughout the process to make it easy.

Sign in

Once you have your Bitcoin Lightning Network node running, sign in with a signed message.

Apply to a Swap

Make sure you have enough SATs to join a liquidity swap. You can also start a new Swap if you like.

Open channel

When the Swap is full with participants we will notify you, so you can open your channel.

Rate participants

Enjoy your new incoming channel and rate your experience.
